    Our Story

  • March 2011 - IDS Nigeria was incorporated.

  • June 2012 - IDS Nigeria started operations.

  • March 2013 - IDS relocated it's operational HQ from Lagos Nigeria to Accra Ghana.

  • December 2013 - IDS was fully incorporated in Ghana as a marketing research and consulting firm.

  • October 2015 - IDS relocated back it's HQ to Lagos Nigeria.

  • January 2016- IDS opened a small office in Asaba, Delta State, Nigeria to cover and cater for our operations in South East and South-South Nigeria.

  • July 2018 - IDS opened CATI office in Abuja.

  • January 2019 - IDS close down it's Lagos Office and move all it's Nigeria operations to Abuja new CATI office.

  • March 2019 - IDS Africa ultra-modern 50 CATI stations office was opened in response to our increasing CATI projects demands.
